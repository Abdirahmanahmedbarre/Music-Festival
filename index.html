About Us

Learn more about the Somali Music Festival, its mission, and the team behind it!

Our Mission

The Somali Music Festival celebrates the rich and diverse musical traditions of Somalia. Our mission is to bring together artists, musicians, and enthusiasts to experience the beauty of Somali music, dance, and culture in a vibrant and inclusive environment. We aim to create a platform for cultural exchange, artistic expression, and community building.

Our Vision

We envision a world where Somali music and culture are recognized and celebrated globally. Through this festival, we aspire to promote cultural understanding, foster artistic growth, and support Somali talent, while preserving our rich musical heritage for future generations.


First Festival (2010)

The inaugural Somali Music Festival took place in 2010, bringing together musicians and artists from around the world to celebrate Somali heritage through music and dance.

International Collaborations (2015)

In 2015, the festival expanded its reach internationally, collaborating with artists from various countries to further promote Somali music on a global stage.

Record Attendance (2019)

In 2019, the Somali Music Festival reached a record attendance of over 10,000 visitors, making it the largest cultural gathering for Somali music worldwide.

Artists

Suldaan Seeraar

Suldaan Seeraar is a renowned Somali singer and songwriter, widely recognized for his powerful voice and contributions to Somali music. He gained popularity with his unique blend of traditional Somali melodies and modern rhythms. Known for his emotional and heartfelt lyrics, Seeraar's music often addresses themes of love, culture, and social issues. He has become a prominent figure in the Somali music industry, captivating audiences both locally and internationally.

Kiin Jaamac

Kiin Jaamac is a talented Somali singer, known for her soulful voice and captivating performances. With a rich background in traditional Somali music, she has gained a strong following for her ability to fuse cultural sounds with contemporary music styles. Kiin Jaamac’s songs often touch on themes of love, empowerment, and Somali heritage. She is celebrated for her ability to evoke deep emotions through her music and has earned a reputation as one of the leading female voices in Somali music.

Nimcaan Hilaac

Nimcaan Hilaac is a prominent Somali singer and musician, celebrated for his unique voice and dynamic stage presence. His music blends modern pop, rap, and traditional Somali sounds, appealing to a wide range of audiences. Hilaac is known for his thought-provoking lyrics that often tackle social and political issues, as well as personal stories of love and struggle. His innovative style has made him a major figure in contemporary Somali music, and his popularity continues to grow both in Somalia and among the Somali diaspora.
